About

Built by someone who
has done the work.

Stephanie Cohen
Founder & Principal, Urban Core AI · Denver, CO

Urban Core was founded on a simple observation after years of working inside and alongside professional services firms: most don't have a technology problem. They have a process and signal problem.

Data lives in silos. Business development is relationship-driven but poorly tracked. The tools exist — CRM, project management, marketing platforms — but they're fragmented, underused, or built without accounting for how people actually work.

I built Urban Core to close that gap — with AI and automation systems that reflect the realities of long sales cycles, complex stakeholder relationships, and the human dynamics of professional services.

No generic transformation frameworks. No dashboards that look good in a demo and go unused after 90 days. Just operational leverage — the kind that compounds.
